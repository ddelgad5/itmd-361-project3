About Me

My name is David Delgado. I am a third year student at Illinois Tech, but only three semesters into the Information Technology and Management (ITM) program. Originally, I was majoring in electrical engineering, but the math is not for the feint of heart. I am specializing in systems administration and minoring in computer science. I am a commuter student and it takes about an hour twenty to get from Jefferson Park to Bronzeville via the CTA. Although, it's really difficult to get involved in the organizations here, I run a radio show here on campus. You can find out more about my show on my music page!

I am a native of Chicago and I have this unexplainable love for the city. For a majority of my life, I've resided in the 60630 area. My most favorite hobby to do in the city is biking. There's this inexplicable rush you get when your dodging cars on the Mag Mile, dodging people on the Chicago Lakefront Trail, or just taking in the view of the forest on the North Branch Trail (technically this is Chicagoland).
